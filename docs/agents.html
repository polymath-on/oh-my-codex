Agent Catalog

Public task-intent surface plus the internal expert prompt catalog.

The primary review/analysis surface is intentionally task-shaped: use $analyze for investigation, $code-review for umbrella review, and /prompts:critic as a first-class public critique agent. This is a mixed invocation model by design: $analyze and $code-review are public skill routers, while the prompt catalog below exposes the internal experts they route into.

Public Review/Analysis Entry Points

Entry PointTypeRoutes ToPurpose
$analyzeskill/prompts:architect or /prompts:debuggerTask-intent investigation entry that chooses structural diagnosis vs failure isolation.
$code-reviewskill/prompts:code-reviewer with /prompts:security-reviewer escalationPublic umbrella review entry for quality, API, performance, style, and security-sensitive follow-up.
/prompts:criticpromptdirectFirst-class public critique agent for plans and designs when critique itself is the task.
